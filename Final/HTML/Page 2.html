How To Start A Gaming Blog

Make a Plan

The first step when you start your own gaming blog can often take the longest. All through life it's important to plan and prepare, and blogging is no exception. If you take the time now to get all your ideas down on paper, you'll avoid distractions and stay focused as you make your way through the blog setup process.

Why Do You Want to Blog?

Your "why" should always guide your decisions, so it's really important to be as clear as you can on this from the beginning. Do you want to inspire others? Create a community of passionate fans? Launch a profitable blogging media empire? It's helpful to remember that you can have more than one purpose - it's possible to create a thriving community and be profitable, for example.

What Do You Want to Blog About?

There's lots of talk in the blogging world about finding your niche and how this can help you to find your audience. What this means is that if you can figure out what's unique about your blog and where it fits into the gaming blog community, you'll find it easier to promote your blog and attract the kind of readers that you want. To help you find your niche, work out which aspects of gaming you're passionate about. Do you favor a particular game or type of game? Are you interested in esports? Do you want to blog about the gaming industry? Are you a huge technology and hardware fan? Blogging about your interests is a great place to start, so make a list of the topics or themes you want to cover on your blog.

Who Will Read Your Gaming Blog?

We often start a blog as a hobby, so sometimes we don't always consider our audience. If you want to grow a successful blog and monetize it, your audience becomes your number one priority. Take some time to build up a picture of your ideal reader. Make notes about who they are, where they live, what they're interested in, and where they like to spend their time. This will help you to create high quality content that will interest them and also help you work out where to promote your blog.

What Should You Name Your Gaming Blog?

After you've decided what you want to blog about, the next step is to find a name. This can be tricky, especially as common words and phrases may already be taken. You also have to watch out for any trademarked phrases, especially if you blog about one game, publisher, or platform only. When it comes to naming your blog, the choice is up to you. You could go for a generic, widely-appealing name like That Video Game Blog, or something more creative like Destructoid.
